** Key Business Insights **

Revenue is concentrated among a small number of product lines, indicating product-level dependency.

Sales performance varies across years, suggesting potential seasonality or demand shifts.

A limited number of countries contribute majority of total revenue.

Medium and Large deals drive the bulk of sales value.

Average Order Value indicates opportunities for upselling strategies.

** Strategic Recommendations **

Focus marketing investments on high-performing product lines.

Expand operations in top-performing regions.

Introduce bundled offers to convert small deals into medium-tier deals.

Use historical trends to guide inventory planning and forecasting.
